## Content Writing Tips

### Writing Your Bio
- Lead with your current status/identity
- Explain how you got here (briefly)
- Share what drives you
- Include specific examples
- End with current focus or goals

### Describing Projects
- Start with what it does
- Explain the challenge or goal
- Highlight your solution
- Mention technologies used
- Share results or learnings

### Listing Skills
- Be honest about proficiency
- Focus on what you use regularly
- Include both technical and soft skills
- Show you're still learning
- Provide context where helpful

### Contact Page Tone
- Friendly and welcoming
- Clear and direct
- Professional but approachable
- Show you're excited to connect
- Make it easy to reach you
