About Me

Developer, learner, and builder of things on the web

Who am I?

Hi, I'm Tristan Thomas, a web developer based in North Idaho. I discovered my passion for web development several years ago when I built my first website and realized I could turn creative ideas into functional digital experiences. Since then, I've been on a journey of continuous learning, building projects that challenge me and help me grow as a developer.

Background & Journey

My path to web development started with curiosity and a desire to understand how the internet works. I began with HTML and CSS, creating simple static pages, and gradually expanded my skills to include JavaScript, responsive design, and modern frameworks. Along the way, I've completed numerous online courses, built personal projects, and collaborated with others to bring ideas to life. Each project has taught me something new and reinforced my love for problem-solving through code.

Approach & Philosophy

I believe great websites are built on a foundation of clean, semantic code and user-centered design. I approach every project with attention to detail, a focus on accessibility, and a commitment to writing code that's not just functional, but maintainable and scalable. I'm passionate about creating experiences that are both beautiful and intuitive, where form and function work together seamlessly.

Current Focus

Currently, I'm focused on expanding my JavaScript skills and diving deeper into React and modern web development practices. I'm particularly interested in building interactive web applications that solve real-world problems. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing what I've learned with the developer community.
